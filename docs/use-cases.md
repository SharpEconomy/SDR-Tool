# Use Cases

## 1. Mumbai food brand looking for distributors

Input:

- packaged snack brand
- wants regional distributors and modern trade buyers
- target geographies: Maharashtra and Gujarat
- prefers SMB and mid-market retail groups

Output:

- ranked distributors, retail chains, and channel partners
- reasoning tied to regional relevance, public contact paths, and partnership intent
- skipped sheet showing low-trust or irrelevant listings

## 2. Bengaluru SaaS startup looking for implementation partners

Input:

- B2B SaaS product
- needs channel and service partners
- target geographies: India, UAE, Singapore

Output:

- implementation agencies
- reseller partners
- consulting firms with public partnership signals
- next action for each high-priority target

## 3. Jaipur manufacturer screening supplier options

Input:

- furniture manufacturer
- needs suppliers for hardware and packaging
- excludes unverified factories and weak contact paths

Output:

- supplier shortlist with trust signals
- contact quality summary
- skipped sheet for missing-contact and low-trust entities

## 4. Chennai healthcare services company tracking procurement opportunities

Input:

- healthcare operations service provider
- wants procurement-style opportunities in India
- prefers public listings with clear timing and scope signals

Output:

- procurement and RFQ-style opportunities
- priority based on geography, timing, and expected value
- export for internal follow-up and audit

## 5. Pune D2C brand needing growth vendors

Input:

- direct-to-consumer brand
- needs warehousing, logistics, and activation partners
- budget is balanced, not enterprise-scale

Output:

- service providers and vendors matched to lean-to-balanced budget fit
- accessible contact paths
- explanations that non-technical operators can act on immediately
